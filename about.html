About Us

AECONS stands for Architecture, Engineering, and Construction. Founded in August 2018, this company was born from passion, faith, and a dream. We specialize in pre-design, master planning, design-build, urban design, and general construction services — always site-sensitive and culturally aware.

Firm History

Aecons Architectural Service was established in 2019 in Quezon City by founder Daniel Talion. From day one, it pursued innovation and client-specific consultancy across multiple sectors.

As a sole proprietorship, Aecons earned a reputation for blending creativity with engineering precision. The firm delivers quality residential, commercial, and industrial solutions across the nation.
