About Us

AECONS

AECONS (Architecture, Engineering, and Construction) is a design and construction firm founded in 2018 by Daniel Talion. Based in Quezon City, we deliver innovative and sustainable solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects nationwide, specializing in Pre-Design, Master Planning, Design-Build, Urban Design, and General Construction.

Commitment

Established in 2019 under the leadership of Daniel Talion, Aecons quickly earned recognition for innovative and high-quality architectural and engineering solutions. With a skilled team and a strong focus on sustainability, we continue to thrive as a trusted name in the industry.
